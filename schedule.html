Schedule

Here is the schedule for the event:

  • Event Day 1: Technical Symposium

    • Dive into the world of innovation and knowledge exchange as Synergy Fest kicks off with a Technical Symposium. Renowned speakers from various industries will grace the stage to deliver insightful talks on cutting-edge technologies, entrepreneurship, and the future of innovation. Concurrent workshops and hands-on sessions will empower attendees to delve deeper into fields like artificial intelligence, blockchain, and sustainable engineering. The day concludes with a captivating hackathon where brilliant minds collaborate to solve real-world problems.

  • Event Day 2: Sports Extravaganza

    • Gear up for a day filled with adrenaline-pumping action at the Sports Extravaganza. Athletes from across the campus will compete in a plethora of sporting events, ranging from traditional favorites like football and basketball to niche sports like ultimate frisbee and rock climbing. Spectators can cheer on their favorite teams while enjoying delicious snacks from food stalls lining the sports grounds. The spirit of camaraderie and healthy competition will be palpable throughout the day.

  • Event Day 3: Cultural Fiesta

    • Embark on a journey of diversity and celebration at the Cultural Fiesta, where the rich tapestry of our college community comes to life. Experience a vibrant showcase of music, dance, art, and cuisine from around the globe as talented performers take the stage to mesmerize audiences with their cultural expressions. Interactive cultural booths offer a glimpse into the traditions and heritage of different countries, inviting attendees to immerse themselves in a melting pot of cultures. The evening culminates in a grand gala dinner featuring an eclectic spread of international delicacies.
